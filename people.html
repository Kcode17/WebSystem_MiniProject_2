Rise and Fall of Napster

Shawn Fanning and John Fanning are the brains behind Napster. Napster was a service which enabled peer-peer music file transfers.It came into existence in 1999, when music was being sold as stores in the form of CDs and records.This service was extremely popular, it provided access to free mp3 music files to all its members, basically it was free song files being shared among all the members. At one point of time, Napster had 80 million users registered and almost all kinds of music was available right from Dre tracks to Mettalica.

The Napster service was short-lived, as many record labels and the music industry was against it as copy-righted content was being shared at zero cost.RIAA (Recording Industry Association of America) had filed a lawsuit over Napster for the unauthorized distribution of copy-righted material.

Youtube rising

Youtube was over the internet and was registered as "Youtube" in 2005. It was one of a kind video platform where people could watch and post videos. Television media was no more the only medium for mass communication.YouTube laid the groundwork and contributed to the growth and popularisation of on-demand online video streaming with its service evolving over the years. It also transformed advertising models for the media and entertainment sector.Communication and collaboration was on the rise due to platforms like youtube.

Social Media - Facebook

Interacting with friends and family across long distances has been a concern of humans. As social beings, people have always depended on communication to strengthen their relationships.This is where social media took the world by storm.

Facebook and Myspace were the two big names when we talk about social media and connecting with people.While MySpace was originally wildly popular, Facebook overtook MySpace in terms of membership in 2008. Facebook was started my Mark Zuckerburg in his dorm room. It was initially used among college students inside Havard and slowly spread to all other colleges over a period of time.Facebook created a way of maintaining relationships over the web with features like chat,like,share with friends. It was an instant hit.
